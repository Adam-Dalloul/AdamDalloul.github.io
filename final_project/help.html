Let Us Help

Nourishing the hungry with care

First Time Client Information

  • Everyone is welcome to shop with us!
  • You can visit one time per week.
  • On your first visit, you will fill out a small piece of paperwork about you and your family members.
  • We do not have appointments. We begin distributing line numbers 30 minutes before we open. Our busiest time is the first half-hour to hour after we open. We are constantly restocking throughout the day!
  • Our goal is to provide about half of a family’s food needs for a week – you will receive 2-4 bags depending upon your family size.
  • We recommend you bring your own reusable shopping bags.
  • Masks are optional.

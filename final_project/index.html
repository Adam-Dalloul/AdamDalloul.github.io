Come on in!

We've modified our distribution process to maintain physical distancing and minimize food handling while allowing indoor shopping!

  • Hand sanitizers at the door. Masks are optional.
  • Numbers are distributed 30 mins before opening
  • Receive a color-coded shopping basket according to the number of people in your family.
  • Shop our aisles while maintaining social distance.
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables, proteins and bread are picked with volunteer support
  • Pack your own bags at sanitized tables
  • Come once a week!

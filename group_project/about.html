Who are we?

How It All Started

At Eastside Preparatory School, three high school students named Yash, Adam, and Rohan found themselves immersed in their studies and discovering a shared passion for education. They often helped their friends and classmates with difficult subjects, and their ability to explain concepts in a relatable way was well-known among their peers. They realized that they all derived immense satisfaction from helping others succeed academically and wanted to expand their impact beyond their immediate circle of friends. Inspired by their conversations, Yash, Adam, and Rohan decided to start a tutoring business together.

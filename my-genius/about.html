Who am I?

I'm Adam, a student at Eastside Preparatory School (EPS). EPS challenges me academically while encouraging me to pursue my personal goals. I enjoy playing Golf (most of all), Soccer, participating in business clubs, and pursuing outside of schools hobbies, like my love for technology. I am grateful for the opportunities EPS has provided me and hope to continue making the most of my time there.
